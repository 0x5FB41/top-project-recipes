Martabak Terang Bulan Recipe

Description

Martabak Terang Bulan, also known as "sweet martabak" or "martabak manis," is a popular Indonesian street food that is a favorite treat for many. It is a type of thick, sweet pancake filled with a variety of delicious toppings and fillings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter for spreading
  • Sugar for sprinkling
  • Condensed milk
  • Chocolate sprinkles or Nutella
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make the Batter:
    • In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    • In another bowl, whisk the egg and milk together, then add the melted butter.
    • Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients, stirring until smooth.
    • Let the batter rest for about 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Pancake:
    • Heat a large, non-stick pan over medium heat.
    • Pour the batter into the pan, spreading it evenly to form a thick pancake.
    • Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges start to set.
    • Cover the pan with a lid and cook for another few minutes until the pancake is cooked through and the bottom is golden brown.
  3. Add the Fillings:
    • Remove the pancake from the pan and place it on a flat surface.
    • Spread a generous amount of butter over the hot pancake.
    • Sprinkle sugar evenly over the butter.
    • Drizzle with condensed milk.
    • Add chocolate sprinkles or Nutella, crushed peanuts, and grated cheese if using.
  4. Fold and Serve:
    • Fold the pancake in half to sandwich the fillings.
    • Cut into wedges or squares for serving.
    • Enjoy the Martabak Terang Bulan warm for the best flavor and texture.

Enjoy!
